Sahil

The quiet one with the flat cap. Don't let the calm fool you — there's a storm of Swift underneath.

Page 1

Everyone else is talking. Sahil is shipping. By the time the debate ends, he's already pushed the commit.

Page 2

The bug was three layers deep in an optional chain. Sahil found it by reading the code top to bottom. No debugger. No print statements. Just reading. Like a book.

Page 3

'This will crash on iPad.' They didn't believe him. They tested it. It crashed on iPad. Sahil adjusted his glasses and said nothing. He never needs to.

Page 4

While the office sleeps, Sahil reads the documentation others skip. Tomorrow he'll show them something they didn't know was possible. Tonight, he tips his cap and walks home.
