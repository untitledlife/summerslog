Cyril

If the office runs, Cyril is running it. DevOps, logistics, team lunches, event planning — everything ops means *everything*.

Page 1

Ask Cyril what his job is and he'll laugh. 'Everything.' The servers. The lunch orders. The event logistics. The thing that broke at 3 AM. Everything.

Page 2

CPU at 95%, 12 lunch orders, and 30 chairs to set up. Cyril doesn't prioritize — he parallelizes. The laugh? That's his load balancer.

Page 3

The dashboard went red at 4 PM on a Friday. By 4:07, it was green. Cyril doesn't panic — panic is just a ticket that hasn't been triaged yet.

Page 4

The event runs perfectly. Nobody thanks the ops guy — because when ops is good, nobody notices ops. But tonight, someone raises a glass. He covers his face, laughing. That's just how Cyril takes a compliment.
